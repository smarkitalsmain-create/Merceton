# **SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY**

**Merceton**

Last Updated: 28 February 2026

Merceton is a technology platform operated by Smarkitals Technologies India Private Limited that enables independent merchants to sell products online.

Merceton does not directly manufacture, stock, or sell products unless explicitly stated. Each merchant is responsible for fulfilling orders placed through their storefront.

## **1\. Shipping Models**

Orders placed through merchant storefronts on Merceton may be fulfilled through:

1. Merchant Direct Shipping  
    The merchant ships products directly to the customer.

OR

2. Integrated Logistics Partners  
    The merchant may use courier partners integrated into the Merceton platform (such as Delhivery or other supported providers).

**2\. Order Processing Time**

Order processing time is determined by the individual merchant and may vary depending on:

* Product availability  
* Merchant location  
* Operational capacity

Processing time is typically 1–3 business days unless otherwise specified by the merchant.

## **3\. Delivery Timeline**

Delivery timelines depend on:

* Shipping method selected  
* Courier partner network  
* Delivery destination

Estimated delivery typically ranges between 3–10 business days within India, depending on location.

Merceton does not guarantee specific delivery timelines.

## **4\. Shipping Charges**

Shipping charges are determined by:

* Merchant pricing policies  
* Selected courier partner  
* Delivery location

Shipping charges, if applicable, will be displayed at checkout.

**5\. Delays**

Delays may occur due to:

* Courier disruptions  
* Weather conditions  
* Public holidays  
* Regulatory restrictions

Merceton is not liable for delivery delays caused by third-party logistics providers.

## **6\. Order Tracking**

If the merchant uses integrated logistics partners, tracking details may be made available through:

* Merchant storefront  
* Courier tracking link

Tracking availability depends on the selected courier service.

## **7\. Responsibility**

The merchant is solely responsible for:

* Timely dispatch  
* Proper packaging  
* Accurate shipping details  
* Compliance with shipping regulations

Merceton facilitates logistics integrations but is not responsible for product handling after dispatch.
